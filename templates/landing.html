About Mfuko Yangu

Mfuko Yangu is designed to help you manage your finances efficiently. With features such as account registration, secure login, deposit, withdrawal, peer-to-peer transfers, and transaction history, the app provides a comprehensive solution for all your banking needs. Whether you want to deposit funds, transfer money to friends, or keep track of your transactions, Mfuko Yangu offers a convenient and secure platform to manage your money with ease.
